## Description

Znap is a powerful, lightweight screenshot and screen recording tool for macOS. Capture, annotate, and share your screen in seconds — all from the menu bar.

**Capture Everything**
- Area, window, and fullscreen screenshots with customizable shortcuts
- Freeze your screen to capture precise moments
- Scrolling capture for full-page content
- Instant OCR — select any text on screen and copy it to clipboard

**Record Your Screen**
- High-quality screen recording with system audio
- Pause and resume recordings
- Built-in video editor with trim, split, and segment deletion
- Visual timeline with draggable split markers

**Annotate Like a Pro**
- Arrows, rectangles, circles, lines, and freehand drawing
- Text and handwriting annotations
- Auto-incrementing step counters for tutorials
- Blur, pixelate, and spotlight tools for sensitive content
- Full undo/redo, selection, move, and resize

**Beautiful Backgrounds**
- Place screenshots on gradient or solid color backgrounds
- Adjustable padding, corner radius, and shadow
- Optional macOS window header with traffic lights
- Aspect ratio presets (16:9, 4:3, 1:1, etc.)

**Fast & Lightweight**
- Lives in your menu bar — no dock clutter
- Instant access with global keyboard shortcuts
- Appears in CMD+TAB when editing
- Auto-update via Sparkle

## Privacy Policy

Znap does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All captures are stored locally on the user's device. No analytics, no telemetry, no network requests (except Sparkle update checks to GitHub).
